# Catalog Research — Premium Women's Range Expansion (SKIMS / Athleta / H&M)

Research only — no catalog changes yet. This studies the reference brands + 2026 premium
women's trends to propose *what to add* on top of the current 20 products. Original names/copy
only; brands used purely as design inspiration (no trademarks/assets copied).

## 1. What the references sell (studied)

**SKIMS** — Clothing, Loungewear (Soft Lounge), Bodysuits, Swim, Bras, Shapewear.
- Signature silhouettes: **Soft Lounge slip dress & long-sleeve dress**, **cotton-rib tank / boxer**, **sculpt brief & thong bodysuits**, **long-sleeve / mock-neck / cami / t-shirt bodysuits**, **jumpsuits**, **refined-knit sets**, **cotton-jersey flares**, **woven-cotton cargo pants**, **halter unitard ("smooth layers")**.
- Swim: **triangle / halter / scoop bikini tops**, **tie-side bottoms**, **scoop-neck & plunge one-pieces**. Tech fabric = polyamide/elastane.
- Palette: rich neutrals (nude/mocha/onyx/clay/sand) + occasional pop (pink, red).

**Athleta ("Transcend" line studied)** — **tube top**, **high-rise stirrup legging**, **wrap crop top**, **scoop sports bra**, **high-rise 7/8 & stash-pocket leggings**, tennis dresses, one-pieces. Fabric stories around buttery/second-skin performance knits.

**H&M women's tops (premium/elevated end)** — beyond basics: **ribbed tanks, tube/bandeau tops, halter tops, wrap tops, corset/bustier tops, mesh & cut-out tops, cropped cardigans, bodysuits**.

## 2. 2026 premium trend signals (research-backed)

- **Flares & wide-leg** have overtaken slim leggings; **stirrup leggings** and **capri/crop** lengths are resurgent (70s revival).
- **One-piece bodysuits & unitards**, **matching short/lounge sets**, **longline bras** worn as tops.
- **Soft-sculpt compression** (SKIMS-style shaping) is core.
- **Cargo & utility** bottoms crossing into athleisure.
- Colour: **rich neutrals** (beige, mocha, ivory) dominate; **pops of red / retro green / deep blue** and pastels as accents.
- Athleisure = "flare pant + longline bra under an open layer," not "leggings + hoodie."

## 5. Colour palette (recommend extending)

Keep the 6 neutrals (Onyx, Chalk, Soft Sage, Sandstone, Espresso, Slate) and add 2 SKIMS-style tones + 1 pop for range: **Clay / Cocoa** (nudes) and **Poppy** (red accent) — trend-aligned.

## 6. Imagery direction (the current gap)

References use **clean studio, full-length on-model, per-colour, 3–4 angles** on white/greige. Our data model supports this (per-colour galleries), but our images are Unsplash stand-ins that don't match garments. For a true luxury look we need **real per-garment/per-colour photography** — either supplied assets or an AI product-image pipeline for consistent on-model shots.

## 7. Decisions needed before building

1. **Swim** — add the category (bikinis/one-pieces) or skip? (It's revealing — your call.)
2. **Scale** — add all ~18, or a curated subset?
3. **Palette** — extend with Clay/Cocoa/Poppy, or keep 6?
4. **Imagery** — supply real photos, approve an AI image pipeline, or keep stand-ins for now?
